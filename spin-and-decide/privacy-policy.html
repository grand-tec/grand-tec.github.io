The core functionality of Spin & Decide (adding options and spinning the wheel) happens locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or contacts.

However, we use third-party advertising services to support the app, which may automatically collect certain information.

We do not control Google's data collection practices. For more information on how Google uses this data, please review the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.


Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties ourselves. The only data shared is the automated diagnostic and advertising data collected directly by the third-party services mentioned above.
